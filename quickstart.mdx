Welcome to AniDev! This guide will walk you through creating your account and watching your first anime on our modern streaming platform.

## What is AniDev?

AniDev is a modern anime streaming and exploration platform that offers:

- High-quality anime streaming with advanced video player
- Dynamic search with multiple filters (genres, studios, ratings, year, season)
- Personal collections and watch history tracking
- Schedule system for upcoming anime releases
- Personalized user experience with custom themes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Visit [anidev.app](https://anidev.app) and click the **Sign Up** button in the navigation bar.

    Fill in the registration form:
    - **Username**: Choose a unique username for your profile
    - **Email**: Your email address for account verification
    - **Password**: A secure password for your account

    <Note>
      AniDev uses Supabase Authentication with PKCE flow for secure account creation. Your credentials are encrypted and protected.
    </Note>

    Once you submit the form, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Profile">
    After signing in, navigate to your profile page at `/profile`.

    **Customize your profile:**
    - **Avatar**: Upload a profile picture using the drag-and-drop interface with built-in image cropping
    - **Username**: Update your display name if needed
    - **Theme**: Choose your accent color to personalize the interface
    - **Parental Control**: Toggle content filtering for family-friendly viewing

    <Tip>
      Your profile preferences are automatically saved and synced across all your devices.
    </Tip>

    **Create your collections:**
    - **Collection**: Your main anime library
    - **Watching**: Track animes you're currently watching
    - **Completed**: Mark animes you've finished
    - **To Watch**: Plan your next anime adventure
  </Step>

  <Step title="Discover and Watch Your First Anime">
    Now you're ready to explore AniDev's extensive anime library!

    **Browse the homepage:**
    - The homepage showcases curated anime sections including trending, top-rated, and seasonal releases
    - Each anime card displays the title, cover image, genres, and rating

    **Use advanced search:**
    1. Click the search icon or navigate to `/search`
    2. Use filters to narrow your search:
       - **Genres**: Action, Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy, and more
       - **Year**: Filter by release year
       - **Status**: Currently Airing, Finished Airing, or Upcoming
       - **Studios**: Search by animation studio
       - **Rating**: Filter by user ratings

    **Watch an anime:**
    1. Click on any anime card to view its details page
    2. Browse the episode list, watch trailers, and read reviews
    3. Click "Watch" or select an episode to start streaming
    4. Enjoy high-quality playback with the Vidstack player featuring:
       - Quality selection
       - Episode navigation
       - Progress tracking (automatically saved to your watch history)
       - Keyboard shortcuts for controls

    <Note>
      Your watch progress is automatically tracked. Return anytime and pick up right where you left off!
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Additional Features">
    **Schedule System:**
    - Visit `/schedule` to view a calendar of upcoming anime releases
    - Never miss a new episode of your favorite shows

    **Advanced Filtering:**
    - Use the debounced search for instant results as you type
    - Combine multiple filters for precise anime discovery

    **Collections Management:**
    - Add animes to your collections from any anime detail page
    - Drag and drop to reorder your collection
    - View your complete watch history in your profile

    <Tip>
      Enable notifications to get updates when new episodes of your watched animes are released.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
